The Game of Golf

This web page provides information about the game of golf. This includes a little about it's origins, main rules and other background. The game originated in it's current form in Scotland, but the Netherlands claim an earlier version originated in their country. We may never know the precise origins.

Golf is typically played on a golf course consisting of either 9 or 18 holes, usually played on grass. Each hole begins on the tee and ends on a green, a closely mown area with a hole in the ground. The player moves the ball from the tee to the hole on the green using one or more golf clubs. The objective is to get the ball into the hole in as few shots as possible, without injuring yourself or other people and without damaging property.

Some examples of prominent golf courses are:

  • St Andrews
  • Pebble Beach
  • Carnoustie

St Andrews Old Course is probably the most famous golf course.
